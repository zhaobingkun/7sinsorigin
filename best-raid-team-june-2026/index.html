Best Raid Team June 2026

The best raid team in June 2026 is not simply the highest-damage lineup. Version 1.5 raid is weekly-limited content, so the strongest team is the one that clears reliably, covers roles cleanly, and does not collapse when mechanics punish greedy builds.

Best Raid Team Rules

  • Start with one durable main damage lane, not two half-built carries
  • Keep one clear utility or support slot that protects consistency under pressure
  • Do not overfit for burst if it weakens weekly clear reliability

What Weekly Raid Changes

Developer Notes #13 describe Raid as weekly content with rewards capped at two clears per week. That means the best team is the team that protects those capped rewards, not the one that wins a theoretical damage showcase.

Practical Team Shape

  • Main carry: one hero you can fully support
  • Stable second slot: utility or secondary pressure that does not break rotation
  • Support slot: sustain, buff, or control that prevents wipe risk
  • Flex slot: matchup adjustment, not blind damage greed

When Daisy or Escanor Fit

Daisy becomes easier to justify if you need stability and utility. Escanor becomes easier to justify if your account already supports a clean damage shell and can protect his pressure without sacrificing weekly consistency.

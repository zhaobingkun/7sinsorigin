How to Link Accounts

Account-link pages solve a real setup problem: which login method to use, when to bind the account, and how to avoid splitting progress across the wrong email or platform flow.

Safe Account-Link Order

  1. Decide which login method you actually plan to keep long-term
  2. Check whether the game separates guest, platform, and publisher accounts
  3. Confirm binding before heavy progress or spending begins

Common Mistakes

  • Starting on a guest account and forgetting to bind it
  • Using a spare social login you will not keep
  • Assuming all platform logins merge automatically

Best Companion Pages

This page pairs well with Pre-Register, Release Date, and controller or platform-support pages, because account-link confusion usually appears right before launch or right after install.

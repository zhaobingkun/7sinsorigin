Best Beginner Team

The best beginner team is usually the lineup you can finish, pilot, and keep upgrading without splitting your account in too many directions. That makes stability and resource pressure more important than flashy theory alone.

What Makes a Good Beginner Team

  • A clear main carry with low roster confusion
  • Support slots that are realistic to finish early
  • Stable survival or easy burst timing instead of fragile perfection

Safest Reading Order

Use Best Team Comps and Best Team by Element for lineup ideas, then compare with Diane, King, and Elaine to see which path is most realistic for your account.

When to Reroute

If your intended starter team needs too many contested resources or rare pairings, it is usually better to pick the safer lineup first and revisit the higher-ceiling comp later.

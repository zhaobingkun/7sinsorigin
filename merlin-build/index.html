Merlin Build Guide for Seven Deadly Sins Origin

Build Merlin as a role-first Version 1.5 investment. Until deeper testing settles, the safest path is to upgrade what makes her useful in real teams instead of chasing a fragile showcase build.

Direct Answer

The safest Merlin build in June 2026 is a balanced utility build: raise the skills and gear path that make her consistent in current content first, then tune damage once your raid and weekly clear teams are stable.

Build Priorities

  • Role consistency first: upgrade the part of Merlin that gives your team reliable value every run.
  • Survivability before greed: do not make her the weak link in raid or longer fights.
  • Damage after stability: push offensive tuning only after she already fits your rotation.

What to Avoid

Avoid spending rare materials just because Merlin is the newest SSR. If your account still lacks a stable main carry, core support, or raid shell, keep her build flexible until you know exactly where she fits.

Best Gear Logic

Use gear that supports Merlin's actual job on your account. For raid-focused accounts, consistency and survival are usually worth more than a small damage ceiling. For general progression, choose a setup that works before perfect substats.

When to Stop Investing

  • Stop once Merlin can hold her role in your current team without draining all upgrade materials.
  • Pause before expensive duplicate or rare-resource breakpoints unless she clearly improves weekly rewards.
  • Recheck after the next patch or banner if her role depends on future partners.

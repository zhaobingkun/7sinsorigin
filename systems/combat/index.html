Combat System Explained

Combat pages help players understand how team roles connect. Instead of only listing buttons, the useful approach is to explain burst windows, support timing, role clarity, and why some teams feel safer than others.

How to Read Combat Early

  • Look for who creates the damage window and who cashes it out
  • Track whether a team needs setup before its burst becomes efficient
  • Separate frontline stability from pure ceiling damage

Why Team Pages Matter Here

Combat understanding gets much clearer once you compare lineups. That is why this page should stay tied to Best Team Comps and the character pages instead of sitting alone as a glossary.

Practical Next Step

If you are trying to learn combat fast, open this page first, then compare Diane, Tristan, and Meliodas to see how carry roles may diverge.

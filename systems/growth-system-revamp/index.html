The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin Growth System Revamp Guide

Version 1.5 is not a routine balance patch. Official notes frame it as a broader revamp of core content and the overall growth structure, which means old farming assumptions may become less efficient than they were in May.

What Official Notes Actually Say

  • Developer Notes #13 explicitly describe Version 1.5 as a restructure of the overall growth structure.
  • The stated goal is to streamline combat-facing progression so heroes become easier to build for existing content.
  • The update also ties growth changes to dungeon revamps, easier farming, and gear-system changes rather than treating them as isolated buffs.

What Active Players Should Re-Check

  • Whether current daily energy and farming time still go into the highest-value loop
  • Whether weapon limit-break plans still deserve priority over armor and mastery goals
  • Whether June team plans assume old bottlenecks that Version 1.5 is about to loosen

Why This Search Intent Matters

Players search for growth-system changes when they suspect the old route is no longer optimal. That is exactly the current situation: boss challenge rewards, elite farming, armor logic, and crystalloid distribution are all being repositioned at once.

Best Companion Pages

Pair this page with Progression and Upgrades, Armor Rework and Equipment Sets, Star Trace Crystalloid, and Developer Notes #13.

Practical Direction

The safest read is that Version 1.5 rewards flexible growth planning more than hard commitment to one outdated bottleneck. If you were hoarding around May-era friction points, this is the time to review where those limits are being eased.

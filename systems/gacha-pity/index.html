Gacha and Pity System Explained

This page is not for invented numbers. It is for decision logic: how to read gacha announcements, what pity questions matter, and how to avoid spending before the system is fully clear.

Questions That Actually Matter

  • Is pity shared between banners or isolated per banner?
  • Does pity carry forward after the banner ends?
  • Are featured rates strong enough to justify early spending?

Safe Planning Before Full Details

Until official rates and pity rules are fully stated, the correct approach is conservative. Save more, compare banners against real account needs, and wait for official clarification before treating any community summary as final.

Where to Verify

Use News for patch confirmation, Current Banners for live banner context, and character pages for whether a featured unit is actually worth chasing.

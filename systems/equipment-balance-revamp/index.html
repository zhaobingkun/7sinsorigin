The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin Equipment Balance Revamp Guide

Equipment pages are high-value in Version 1.5 because Netmarble is not just tweaking one underused set. Official notices describe broader equipment balance revamps, unlocked crafting quality ranges, and set-value adjustments that can change real rebuild decisions.

What Official Notices Confirm

  • The equipment notice says Balance Adjustments for some Equipment will be conducted with the Version 1.5 revamp.
  • Crafted equipment and accessories move from fixed quality to a quality range, with higher maximum values becoming obtainable.
  • Developer Notes #13 also say certain armor set values will be adjusted where excessive synergy is expected.

What This Changes for Players

  • Old “never craft this piece” assumptions may no longer hold if quality ceilings improve
  • Set-effect rankings may need to be reviewed once simultaneous equipment interactions settle
  • Salvage, crafting, and rebuild timing all become more sensitive to patch sequencing

Why This Search Intent Matters

Players search for equipment balance when a patch can make yesterday's safe gear logic expensive. That is the current window: if you rebuild too early, you may lock into a weaker route just before values shift.

Best Companion Pages

Pair this page with Armor Rework and Equipment Sets, Growth System Revamp, Progression and Upgrades, and Developer Notes #13.

Practical Direction

The safest approach is to treat Version 1.5 gear planning as a value-reset window. Review your crafting plans, set priorities, and salvaging habits after the new ranges and adjusted synergies are fully live.

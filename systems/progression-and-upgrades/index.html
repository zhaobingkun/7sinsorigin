The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin Progression and Upgrades Guide

Progression pages matter because weak accounts usually do not fail from one bad choice. They fail from scattered upgrades. This guide is about upgrade order, system pacing, and keeping roster, gear, and routine decisions pointed in the same direction.

What Strong Progression Looks Like

  • One clear account goal at a time instead of many half-built lanes
  • Upgrade order that matches current content pressure
  • Daily and weekly loops that support long-term roster growth

Best Use Case

Open this page when the account is active but not efficient. If progress feels slow even after playing regularly, the real issue is often upgrade direction rather than playtime.

Best Companion Pages

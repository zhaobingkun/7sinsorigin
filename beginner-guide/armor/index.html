Seven Deadly Sins Origin Armor Guide

A clean list of early armor sets and piece variants from your provided armor sheet, organized for quick planning.

Beginner Priority

  • Finish one usable 4-piece line first: Top, Bottoms, Combat Boots, Belt.
  • Keep upgrades focused on your main team instead of splitting mats across many sets.
  • Use quality labels as a quick filter: Finest > Rare > Fine > General.

Set Coverage Snapshot

  • Thunderous (Finest): Top, Combat Boots, Bottoms, Belt
  • Star Guardian: Top, Combat Boots, Bottoms, Belt
  • Ruler of the Ocean: Top, Combat Boots, Bottoms, Belt
  • Revived Nightmare: Top, Combat Boots, Bottoms, Belt
  • Pursuer's: Top, Combat Boots, Bottoms, Belt (Rare / General / Fine)
  • Noble Hero's: Top (Rare, Finest)

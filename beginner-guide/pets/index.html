Seven Deadly Sins Origin Pet Guide

A quick pet reference from your provided file with ride speed and unlock-source hints for early progression.

Beginner Pet Priority

  • Get one stable early ride first for travel efficiency.
  • Prioritize higher ground speed when exploration is your current bottleneck.
  • Use acquisition type (Start, Mission, Capture, Challenge) to plan unlock order.

Current Pet Snapshot

  • Fastest ground speed: Ancient Varg of the Sun (Ground Speed 1500)
  • Fastest glide speed: Ancient Howler of the Snowy Mountain / Emerald Flare (Flight Speed 1500)
  • Starter-friendly: Spring Picnic Hawk (Available from the Start)
  • Challenge unlock: Lone Wild Bear
  • Capture route: Domesticated Dusk Bison, Mach

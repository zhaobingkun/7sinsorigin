Seven Deadly Sins Origin Beginner Guide

Fast onboarding guide for new players moving into open-world progression and combat loops.

Day 1 Priority

  • Complete onboarding and unlock core systems
  • Secure stamina/resource flow
  • Build one stable team instead of spreading materials

Resource Priority

  • Main carry upgrades first
  • Essential utility second
  • Avoid over-investing in low-impact gear early

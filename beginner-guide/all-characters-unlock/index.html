SSR Weapon Notes: Black Flame Wings Set

Based on your in-game screenshots, most Black Flame Wings SSR weapons share the same Burst effect: Activating a Burst increases Attack by 28% and Crit Chance by 6% for 10 sec.

  • Main Black Flame Wings types: Grimoire, Dual Swords, Axe, Gauntlets, Rapier, Lance, Staff, Longsword, Greatsword, Sword and Shield, Nunchaku, Wand.
  • Core stat line (shown): ATK 620 with Crit Damage 9.92% for most variants in this set.
  • Notable exception in your list: Gluttonous Soul Longsword (ATK 652, Darkness Attack 462), useful as a separate dark-oriented option.
  • Beginner recommendation: pick one weapon line that matches your main carry's role and keep upgrades focused before branching out.

How to Unlock Characters (Launch Method)

  • Main story unlocks: push chapters first to unlock baseline units and systems.
  • Early missions/tutorial rewards: complete onboarding tasks for guaranteed roster value.
  • Event and login tracks: time-limited campaigns can add free pulls or direct unit access.

Day-1 Character Planning Checklist

  • Pick one core DPS and one stable support before broad investment.
  • Avoid splitting resources across too many untested units.
  • Keep some summon currency for first post-launch balance update.
  • Re-check official notes for final unlock conditions at release.

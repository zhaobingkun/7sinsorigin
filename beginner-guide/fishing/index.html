Seven Deadly Sins Origin Fishing Guide

Fishing pages serve a different search intent than combat or tier-list pages. Players usually want to know whether fishing is worth the time, what it feeds into, and how to fold it into a route without hurting progression.

When Fishing Is Worth It

Fishing is most valuable when it supports a larger loop such as cooking, ingredient stocking, collection progress, or event tasks. If it does not feed a planned objective, it can become a time sink instead of progression support.

Best Beginner Rules

  • Fish during planned gather windows, not in the middle of combat progression.
  • Track which catches actually support cooking or event exchange value.
  • Stop once your current food and task goals are covered.

How Fishing Connects to Other Pages

Fishing naturally links to Cooking Guide, because ingredient planning is the main reason many players search for it. It also fits with Daily Checklist when gathering tasks are part of your reset routine.

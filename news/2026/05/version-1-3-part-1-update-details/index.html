7DS Origin Version 1.3 Part 1 Update: Clotho Banner, New Dungeon Difficulty, and Event Start

Published: May 13, 2026

Direct answer: Version 1.3 Part 1 is the current live update checkpoint players should use when deciding whether to pull for Clotho, re-prioritize dungeon progression, or adjust their first-week growth route. The official update details call out a new hero, new events, and tougher dungeon layers that matter for active accounts.

What changed in Part 1

  • Clotho is the headline addition, with a dedicated pickup banner and matching skin reward line.
  • New event coverage starts with the Clotho pickup cycle and associated event rewards.
  • More difficulty tiers were added to several dungeons and boss-oriented activities, which changes near-term growth priorities.
  • The official notes also preview the next live step, which means current pulls should be judged with Part 2 timing in mind.

What active players should do first

  • Open Current Banner Worth It? if the real question is whether Clotho solves a current account gap.
  • Check Best Banner to Pull On if the decision is less about hype and more about account function.
  • Review How to Farm Diamonds Fast if the update changed your short-term resource path.
  • Use Material Farming and Progression and Upgrades if new dungeon tiers change where you should spend stamina.

Why this update matters more than a simple banner swap

Part 1 is not only a banner notice. It is the point where the live game asks players to evaluate banner value, event time, and progression efficiency together. That means a pull decision without checking current farming routes and upgrade pressure is weaker than usual.

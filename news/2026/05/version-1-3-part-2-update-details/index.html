7DS Origin Version 1.3 Part 2 Update: Timespace Junction, Ranking Missions, and Event Story

Published: May 21, 2026

Direct answer: Version 1.3 Part 2 matters because it adds more than an event wrapper. Official notes point to Timespace Junction expansion, ranking-oriented missions, and a seasonal event story loop that directly changes what active players should farm, compare, and schedule this week.

What changed in Part 2

  • Timespace Junction continues to open out, which pushes longer-horizon progression decisions.
  • Ranking Mission - To the Top adds leaderboard and event-shop pressure, making account efficiency more important.
  • Echoes of Hearts becomes the new event-story anchor for the current cycle.
  • Quality-of-life and matchmaking-related improvements were also tied to this update window, which matters for active routine play.

What active players should re-check now

  • Open Daily Checklist and Weekly Checklist if Part 2 changes your current routine order.
  • Use How to Get Stronger and Progression and Upgrades if Timespace Junction now matters more than your earlier farming lane.
  • Check Current Tier List and Best Team by Element if ranking content changes who you should build first.
  • Open Server Reset Times if the new event loop makes timing and reset efficiency more valuable.

Why Part 2 is a different kind of update

Part 1 mainly asked whether players should spend now or hold. Part 2 asks something broader: whether your current account route can handle event pressure, leaderboard incentives, and deeper sector unlocks without wasting growth resources on the wrong lane.

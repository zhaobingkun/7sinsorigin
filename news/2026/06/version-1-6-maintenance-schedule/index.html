Version 1.6 Maintenance Completed

Published: June 23, 2026 · Official notice checked: June 25, 2026

Direct answer: the official Version 1.6 maintenance notice is now marked completed. The maintenance window was June 25, 2026 from 02:00-09:00 UTC. The update adds Elizabeth, Raid Pledge, Pet Expedition, Taranis's Sanctuary Hell difficulty, 100-day events, and maintenance rewards.

Maintenance and Rewards

  • Maintenance date: June 25, 2026.
  • Maintenance time: 02:00-09:00 UTC, for a planned 7-hour window.
  • Maintenance compensation: Star Fragment x300.
  • AMA Season 2 rewards: Sunstone x10, Malachite x10, and Brilliant Potion of Charm x10.
  • Compensation and AMA rewards can be claimed from the mailbox until July 9, 2026 at 14:59 UTC.

New 1.6 Content

  • New hero Elizabeth is added in Version 1.6.
  • New Elizabeth exclusive skin: The Princess Who Regained Her Memory.
  • Vision of the Azure Waves skin draw includes summer Outfit Skins for SSR Merlin and SSR Clotho.
  • Ranking Season 1, Raid Pledge, Pet Expedition, and Taranis's Sanctuary Hell difficulty are added.
  • Raid Pledge adds raid restrictions for bonus Mystic Tokens and a higher equipment quality range; Mystic Keys reset during the June 25 maintenance.

Events and Sales Changing

  • New events include Hero Elizabeth Trial, Special 100-Day Celebration Check-In, Return of the Sins, A Midsummer Day's Dream, and Gotcha! The Scurry Squad Expedition.
  • Hawk Pass Season III and new 100-day bundles open with the update.
  • Merlin Pick Up, Daisy Pick Up, Merlin Trial, and Raid Missions - Conqueror's Path were extended until before the June 25 maintenance.
  • Leaderboard Pre-Season 2 and Hawk Pass II still end on June 24 at 06:59:59 UTC.

What This Means Now

Use the Version 1.6 Update Details page for Elizabeth banner mechanics. Current Banners should now point to Elizabeth and Radiant Summer Memories instead of the Merlin/Daisy 1.5 ending banners.

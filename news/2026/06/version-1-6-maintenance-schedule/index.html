Version 1.6 Maintenance Schedule

Published: June 23, 2026 · Official notice checked: June 23, 2026

Direct answer: the official Version 1.6 maintenance notice is now live. Maintenance is scheduled for June 24, and Netmarble says the detailed time will be added later in the same notice. The update adds Elizabeth, Raid Pledge, Pet Expedition, Taranis's Sanctuary Hell difficulty, 100-day events, and maintenance rewards.

Maintenance and Rewards

  • Maintenance date: June 24, 2026.
  • The detailed maintenance time has not been posted yet.
  • Maintenance compensation: Star Fragment x300.
  • AMA Season 2 rewards: Sunstone x10, Malachite x10, and Brilliant Potion of Charm x10.
  • Compensation and AMA rewards can be claimed from the mailbox until July 8, 2026 at 14:59 UTC.

New 1.6 Content

  • New hero Elizabeth is added in Version 1.6.
  • New Elizabeth exclusive skin: The Princess Who Regained Her Memory.
  • Vision of the Azure Waves skin draw includes summer Outfit Skins for SSR Merlin and SSR Clotho.
  • Ranking Season 1, Raid Pledge, Pet Expedition, and Taranis's Sanctuary Hell difficulty are added.
  • Raid Pledge adds raid restrictions for bonus Mystic Tokens and a higher equipment quality range; Mystic Keys reset during the June 24 maintenance.

Events and Sales Changing

  • New events include Hero Elizabeth Trial, Special 100-Day Celebration Check-In, Return of the Sins, A Midsummer Day's Dream, and Gotcha! The Scurry Squad Expedition.
  • Hawk Pass Season III and new 100-day bundles open with the update.
  • Merlin Pick Up, Daisy Pick Up, Merlin Trial, Raid Missions - Conqueror's Path, and Leaderboard Pre-Season 2 are scheduled to end around the June 24 maintenance.

What This Means Now

Today this is a maintenance and preview notice, not the final Version 1.6 update-details page. Use it to plan around the June 24 reset, check the Upcoming Banners page for Elizabeth planning, and keep Current Banners on Merlin/Daisy until maintenance actually changes the live banner state.

Official source: Version 1.6 Maintenance Schedule.

Version 1.6 Known Issues Updated June 30: PS5 Quest, Cube Keys, Steam, and Spray Island

Published: June 25, 2026 · Updated: June 30, 2026 05:24 UTC

Direct answer: The June 30 Version 1.6 known-issues update adds two more active issues: the PS5 version may block progress on the Memories in the Spray - Take a photo with Elizabeth quest, and the game may crash while adjusting exchange quantity in the Exchange Cube Keys pop-up when it appears because Cube Keys are insufficient and a controller is being used. Earlier entries still include Steam error 30021225-0, Spray Island event progression issues, key-input failures, and Quality 130+ equipment refining.

June 30 additions

  • On PlayStation 5, the Memories in the Spray quest step to take a photo with Elizabeth cannot be progressed.
  • While using a controller, the game may intermittently crash when adjusting exchange quantity in the Exchange Cube Keys pop-up shown because of insufficient Cube Keys.

June 26 additions

  • Equipment with Quality over 130 cannot be refined.
  • A Midsummer Day's Dream event quest may not appear in the UI or Quest screen.
  • Certain key inputs may intermittently fail to register.
  • Steam error code 30021225-0 can block access; update the Steam build to the latest version.

Spray Island and event issues

  • Event quests may stop progressing after entering the Spray Island event map; reconnecting is the official temporary fix.
  • Infinite loading may occur when matchmaking for Corrupted Guardian Golem after joining a party as a party member from Spray Island; reconnecting is the temporary fix.
  • Controller selection in Return of the Sins can show the Pick Up banner at the top of the list instead of the intended Escanor Pick Up path.

Other active issues to watch

  • Pet Expedition tutorial reset time and bottom UI can display incorrectly.
  • Cooking ingredients may not display at a Campfire until selecting a Recipe and returning to the cooking screen.
  • Leaving a party while in a one-player party at a specific location can move the character to another area; reconnecting is the temporary fix.
  • Special Refinement Stone purchase limit text in the A Midsummer Day's Dream Exchange Shop is displayed as daily, but the official note says it is actually account-limited.
  • Taranis's Sanctuary reward claiming, Act 14 skip behavior, main quest tracker display, and falling under the ground remain listed issues.

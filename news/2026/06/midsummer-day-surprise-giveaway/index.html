Midsummer Day Surprise Giveaway: Daily Weapon Material Rewards

Published: June 29, 2026 · Checked: June 30, 2026

Direct answer: The Midsummer Day Surprise Giveaway runs from June 29 to July 13, 2026 (UTC). Log in during the event to receive daily mailbox rewards for weapon crafting and enhancement. Mail arrives at 09:00 UTC each day and expires at 14:59 UTC the following day.

Event Rewards

  • [Reward Chest] SSR Weapon Fragment Selection Bundle x1
  • [Other] Weapon Crafting Blueprint Fragment x1
  • [Reward Chest] Element Spell Bead Selection Chest x1

Timing to Remember

  • Event window: June 29 through July 13, 2026 (UTC).
  • Mailbox delivery: 09:00 UTC each day.
  • Claim deadline: 14:59 UTC the following day for each daily mail.
  • Because rewards expire quickly, treat this as a daily login check rather than a catch-up event.

Who Should Care Most?

This event is most useful for players still building weapons or trying to stabilize several weapon routes after Version 1.6. If you are evaluating Elizabeth or other current team upgrades, the reward materials can reduce some pressure on weapon crafting and enhancement.

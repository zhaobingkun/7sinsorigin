Developer Notes #15: Version 1.7 QoL, Combat, and Co-Op Plans

Published: June 26, 2026 · Official source: Developer Notes #15

Direct answer: Developer Notes #15 does not announce a new banner. It confirms that damage text visibility improvements are scheduled for Version 1.7, and it says the team is working on clearer Combined Attack guidance, skill-description corrections, item favorites, equipment-salvage information, Time Attack starting-position reviews, raid time-limit monitoring, and future co-op design discussions.

What is confirmed for Version 1.7?

The clearest dated item is damage text visibility. The official note says the team is working to reduce the way overlapping damage numbers hide important boss patterns and effects, with the improvement scheduled for Version 1.7.

Quality-of-life topics

  • Combined Attack explanations are being expanded so basic rules are easier to understand in-game.
  • Skill descriptions that do not match actual behavior are still being corrected.
  • Frequently used items, including Platinum Ore and consumables, may receive a Favorites-style quick-access improvement.
  • Equipment salvage and Engraved Equipment crafting screens are under review for clearer item details and already-crafted indicators.

Combat and end-game topics

  • Time Attack boss starting positions are being reviewed for content such as Corrupted Guardian Golem and Corrupted Warden Orgot, but the note says implementation is not short-term because map content must be coordinated.
  • Raid time limits remain intentional for now, but the team says it will continue watching clear rates, especially after Hell difficulty was added as a more accessible weekly Star Fragment route.
  • Role-based co-op requests are acknowledged, but the current team system makes an immediate DPS/Tank/Support/Healer model difficult.

What players should do now

For today, this is mainly a roadmap and expectation-setting note. If your problem is current gameplay, use the Version 1.6 known issues page. If your decision is about spending diamonds, use Elizabeth banner advice and current banners instead.

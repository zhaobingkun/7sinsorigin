100-Day Anniversary Survey: Star Fragment x300 Reward and Deadline

Published: June 25, 2026 · Survey deadline: July 8, 2026 14:49 UTC

Direct answer: The 100-Day Anniversary Survey runs from after the June 25 maintenance until July 8, 2026 14:49 UTC. The participation reward is Star Fragment x300, distributed through the in-game mailbox after the survey period ends and stored for 30 days after delivery.

How to participate

Use the survey link sent to your in-game mailbox or the official notice's survey link. The reward can only be claimed once per account, so confirm the account, server, character, and UID before submitting.

Reward notes

  • Reward: Star Fragment x300.
  • Schedule: after June 25 maintenance through July 8, 2026 at 14:49 UTC.
  • Reward timing: sequential mailbox delivery after the survey period ends.
  • Mailbox storage: 30 days after delivery.
  • If submitted more than once, the first submission's server, character, and UID are used for reward delivery.

PS5 UID warning

The official notice says the UID copy function is not available on PlayStation 5, so PS5 players should double-check the UID manually before submitting the survey.

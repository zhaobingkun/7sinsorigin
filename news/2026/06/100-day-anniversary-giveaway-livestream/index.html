100-Day Giveaway and Version 1.6 Livestream

Published: June 18, 2026 · Reviewed: June 20, 2026

Direct answer: log in each day from June 18 through June 23 to claim 100 Star Fragments, five Platinum Ingots, and a capturing potion. The Version 1.6 anniversary livestream starts June 23 at 11:00 UTC.

Daily Giveaway

  • Every event day includes 100 Star Fragments and five Platinum Ingots.
  • June 18 and June 21 include one Radiant Potion of Charm.
  • June 19, 20, 22, and 23 include five Concentrated Potions of Charm.
  • Mail arrives at 09:00 UTC and expires at 14:59 UTC the following day.

Version 1.6 Livestream

  • Start: June 23, 2026 at 11:00 UTC.
  • Confirmed highlights: Elizabeth skills and gameplay, Summer Vacation events and skins, and new content.
  • The broadcast runs simultaneously on Korean, Japanese, and Global official YouTube channels, with a replay afterward.

Do Not Miss

Open the game daily before each mailbox reward expires. Pair the event with the Daily Checklist, Star Fragment Guide, Pets Guide, and Events Schedule.

Official sources: 100-Day Anniversary Giveaway Event and 100-Day Anniversary Special Livestream.

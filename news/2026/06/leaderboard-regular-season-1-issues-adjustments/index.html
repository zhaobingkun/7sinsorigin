Leaderboard Regular Season 1 Issues and Content Adjustments

Published: June 30, 2026 · Checked: July 1, 2026

Direct answer: Netmarble found an issue where buffs applied before entering Leaderboard Regular Season 1 content can remain active after entry. A temporary maintenance is planned within the week to block that carryover. The Abyss Galland leaderboard will be reset during that maintenance, Timespace Junction Sector 7 is delayed until after the fix, and Magic Pop is also delayed for additional checks.

Boss Challenge Leaderboard Measures

  • The affected Boss Challenge is [Abyss] Galland of Truth.
  • Temporary maintenance will prevent buffs applied before entry from carrying over after entry.
  • Dishes will be usable while waiting for the content to begin.
  • The entire leaderboard will be reset during the temporary maintenance.
  • Laurel of Glory rewards will be distributed based on ranking status at the start of maintenance.

Delayed Content

  • Timespace Junction Sector 7 is delayed until after the temporary maintenance.
  • Magic Pop - The Day of Mischief Begins! is delayed from its previous July 1 opening because it needs additional checks.

What Players Should Do

Do not treat July 1 as the confirmed opening date for Sector 7 or Magic Pop anymore. Wait for the separate fix-schedule notice before planning leaderboard pushes or event routines around those two pieces of content.

Version 1.6 Update Details: Elizabeth, Events, and Raid Pledge

Published: June 24, 2026 · Official page updated: June 25, 2026 08:13 UTC

Direct answer: Version 1.6 is live after the completed June 25 maintenance. The update adds SSR Elizabeth, the Radiant Summer Memories pickup, Elizabeth and summer skin draws, 100-day celebration rewards, Return of the Sins, A Midsummer Day's Dream, Pet Expedition missions, Hawk Pass Season III, Raid Pledge, and Taranis's Sanctuary Hell difficulty.

Elizabeth and Banner Timing

  • Elizabeth Pick Up runs from after the completed June 25 maintenance until the July 15 maintenance, based on the official update details.
  • The pickup uses Hero Pick Up Draw Tickets, with SSR guaranteed within 80 draws and a 50% pickup chance when an SSR appears.
  • If an SSR is not the pickup hero, the next SSR is guaranteed to be the pickup hero; the 120th draw bonus guarantees the pickup once per banner and does not carry to another banner.
  • Elizabeth has three official weapon routes: Grimoire Holy Warden, Staff Wind Support, and Wand Earth Burst Specialist.

Events and Rewards

  • Special 100-Day Celebration Check-In can give up to 100 Regular Hero Draw Tickets over 14 days.
  • Elizabeth Trial rewards include Star Fragments, Supreme Mastery EXP, Giant Bird Feather, Unnamed Knight's Token, Evening Primrose, and Mermaid's Tear.
  • Return of the Sins grants SSR King and SSR Diane through Main Quest progression, then opens an Escanor pickup window after Act 13 Episode 25.
  • A Midsummer Day's Dream adds Spray Island event content, Golden Wave Tokens, Boat Race content, and the Banakro field boss.

Systems Added in 1.6

  • Raid Pledge adds optional Abyss raid conditions for extra Mystic Tokens and a higher equipment Quality range; the drop rate itself is not increased.
  • Pet Expedition arrives with Gotcha! The Scurry Squad Expedition event missions.
  • Taranis's Sanctuary Hell difficulty is added and, like Abyss, can provide up to 1,000 Star Fragments per week.
  • Timespace Junction Sector 7 opens on July 1 at 07:00 UTC, and Sector 8 opens on July 8 at 07:00 UTC.

June 25 Official Update Note

The official update-details page was updated on June 25 at 08:13 UTC, mainly adding Pet Expedition guide screenshot notes. The maintenance notice is also marked completed, so current-banner routing should now point to Elizabeth instead of Merlin/Daisy.

June 19 Server Patch: Sector 6 Leaderboard Fix

Published: June 19, 2026 · Reviewed: June 20, 2026

Direct answer: the June 19 patch fixed an issue where some Timespace Junction Sector 6 leaderboard records were not displayed. Ranking information may update sequentially, so players should fully restart before checking again.

June 19 Patch

  • Completed June 19 from 08:30 to 08:50 UTC without maintenance.
  • Fixed missing records on the Timespace Junction Sector 6 leaderboard.
  • The patch applies after fully exiting and reconnecting; record visibility may update gradually.

June 16 Patch Context

  • Partially fixed intermittent failures when claiming Taranis's Sanctuary raid-clear rewards.
  • Further improvements are still planned, so unresolved claims should be documented for support.
  • Included additional server stabilization work.

Next Steps

Restart the game before retesting. Use the Timespace Junction update summary, Raid Guide, and Bugs and Errors Hub for related issues.

Official sources: June 19 Server Patch and June 16 Server Patch.

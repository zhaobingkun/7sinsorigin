Official News #92: Launch Phase and Open-World Briefing

Published: March 19, 2026

This page is a concise recap of the official 7origin.netmarble.com update at /en/news/2/92. The key message is the staged rollout and a clearer preview of how exploration and combat are expected to work at launch.

Launch Phase (Official Direction)

  • Initial access opens first for selected platforms before full expansion.
  • The full-service launch then follows across all major target platforms.
  • Netmarble indicates schedule tuning may still happen based on deployment conditions.

What the Update Emphasizes

  • A broad open-world route through Britannia with side content beyond story quests.
  • Traversal variety and interaction-heavy exploration flow.
  • Combat readability and polish informed by player testing feedback.

Player Prep Checklist

  • Complete pre-register and account binding in advance.
  • Follow official channel posts for launch-hour changes and maintenance windows.
  • Review starter team priorities before spending early materials.

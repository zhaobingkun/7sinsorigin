Launch Roadmap and Worldview Briefing

Published: February 26, 2026 | Updated: March 18, 2026

This update summarizes the official rollout message in a cleaner format for players tracking the seven deadly sins: origin. The initial test wave opened first, followed by the broader launch window one week later.

Rollout Timing (Summary)

  • Early access/testing wave began around March 17, 2026.
  • Global release phase was announced for March 24, 2026.
  • Region and platform availability may still differ by local store policy.

World and Exploration Focus

The official preview highlights a cross-timeline setup centered on the Book of Stars. Players travel across areas such as Kingdom of Liones, Fairy King's Forest, Desert Ruins, and Dragon's Grave, each with a different visual tone and enemy pressure.

Combat Direction

The combat loop combines open-field movement with party skill chaining, character swapping, and role-driven loadouts. Team building matters more than single-character damage because encounter pacing shifts between mob clears and boss phases.

Recommended Prep

  • Secure your account and pre-register rewards.
  • Bookmark official channels for patch notices and launch-hour updates.
  • Track character reveals before investing in first-week resources.

Daisy Revealed for 1.1: SSR Rate Up, Hawkpass, and New Events

Published: April 7, 2026

The latest official reveal confirms Daisy as the SSR rate-up character for version 1.1. The same update wave also highlights Hawkpass being officially added, a fresh round of events, and a set of quality-of-life changes arriving with the wider 7DS Origin update package.

Highlights

  • Daisy is officially revealed as the version 1.1 SSR rate-up character.
  • Hawkpass is being officially added as part of the update package.
  • New events are included alongside the banner reveal.
  • Good QOL updates are explicitly mentioned in the official post.

Why It Matters

  • Banner planning becomes clearer for players deciding whether to save or pull in version 1.1.
  • Hawkpass and event additions suggest the update is expanding both routine rewards and live-service activity.
  • QOL improvements may matter just as much as the character reveal for daily players.

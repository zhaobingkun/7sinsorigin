Best Team by Element

Element pages only matter if they help players choose a lane, not just list a lineup. The key question is which element is safest, cheapest, or highest-upside for the roster you actually have.

How to Use Element Teams

  • Pick one element that fits your likely roster and weapon routes
  • Favor teams with a clear carry and easy support completion
  • Treat element choice as a resource decision, not just a flavor choice

When One Element Wins

An element becomes the best path when it gives your account a stable carry, realistic support slots, and fewer contested materials than the alternatives. That is why lineup pages and character pages need to be read together.

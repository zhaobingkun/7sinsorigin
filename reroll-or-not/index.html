Reroll or Not?

Rerolling only makes sense if the time cost is smaller than the long-term account value you gain. For many launch windows, the right answer depends on banner quality, account-link friction, and how much your first team truly changes with one extra unit.

When Rerolling Makes Sense

  • A launch banner has one clearly account-defining unit
  • Account setup and re-entry are fast enough to repeat without heavy friction
  • Your best beginner team changes meaningfully if you land one key role

When It Usually Does Not

  • The banner field is still unstable and the meta is not clear
  • Account linking, install flow, or region setup makes repeated starts painful
  • The game rewards progression speed more than one early gamble

What to Compare First

Check Best Banner to Pull On, Best Beginner Team, and How to Link Accounts before deciding. Reroll advice without account-friction context is weak advice.

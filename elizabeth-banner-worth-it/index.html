Is Elizabeth Worth Pulling in Seven Deadly Sins Origin?

Elizabeth is worth pulling if her Version 1.6 healer, Wind support, or Earth burst routes solve a real account gap. The banner is live after the completed June 25 maintenance and runs until the July 15 maintenance.

Short Answer

Pull Elizabeth if your account needs survival support, Wind-team support, or an Earth burst enabler and you can afford the pickup pity curve. Save if you are low on diamonds, already stable in those roles, or need live testing before spending.

Banner Timing and Pity

  • Event period: after June 25 maintenance until July 15 maintenance.
  • SSR guarantee: within 80 draws.
  • Pickup chance: 50% when an SSR appears.
  • If the SSR is not Elizabeth, the next SSR is guaranteed to be the pickup hero.
  • 120th draw bonus guarantees the pickup once per banner and does not carry over.

Pull If

  • You need a defensive/healing route for hard content reliability.
  • You are building around Wind support or Earth burst teams.
  • You can still keep enough tickets or diamonds for the next known banner cycle after committing.

Save If

Save if your roster already clears current content comfortably, if Merlin or Daisy already drained pity, or if you would only be pulling because Elizabeth is new. Her official kit is promising, but final ranking should wait for live uptime and team testing.

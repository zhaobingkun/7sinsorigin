Upcoming Banners

Use this page for planning, not certainty. Upcoming banner pages should stay close to official reveals, patch notes, and character pages so players can decide whether saving now makes more sense than pulling immediately.

What Counts as Upcoming

  • Official teaser posts and patch-note reveals
  • New character pages that suggest banner relevance
  • Patch cadence that makes a near-term banner likely

Save or Pull Logic

If an upcoming unit is more aligned with your planned team than the current banner, waiting is usually correct. That is especially true if your roster still lacks a stable carry, support glue unit, or banner-defining element core.

Pages to Pair With This One

Upcoming banner planning is strongest when it stays linked to News, Characters, and the tier list. That keeps the page useful even before the banner is officially live.

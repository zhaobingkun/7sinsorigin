The Seven Deadly Sins: Origin Pre-Register

Follow this page when you want the official entry link, the safest registration order, and a short checklist to avoid missing launch rewards or account confirmation steps.

Official Link

Go to the official pre-register page

Use the official page first. Avoid third-party pages that only summarize rewards without a real sign-up flow.

Recommended Order

  1. Open the official pre-register page.
  2. Choose the account option you actually plan to keep.
  3. Confirm the region and the email or social binding flow.
  4. Save the confirmation screen for later support checks.

Reward Checklist

  • Check whether rewards are tied to account creation or a later launch login.
  • Watch official milestone updates instead of assuming every promo graphic is final.
  • Bookmark the news page so you can verify reward changes after each official post.

Common Mistakes

  • Signing up with a throwaway account you do not plan to use at launch
  • Skipping a screenshot or confirmation email check
  • Assuming all regions will receive the same reward timing
  • Missing later follow-up steps after the first sign-up page

What to Prepare Before Launch

After pre-registering, your next useful steps are checking release timing, bookmarking the character overview, and building a short first-week reading list through Beginner Guide, Tier List, and team setup pages.

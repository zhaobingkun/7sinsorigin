Is There Controller Support?

The short answer is: treat controller support as a confirmation page, not an assumption page. Players should verify platform coverage, menu support, and whether native support exists before choosing a setup path.

Short Answer

Controller support is one of the right questions to ask before launch, but support details should be treated as pending until the official game pages, patch notes, or platform announcements make them explicit.

What You Need to Verify

  • Does support exist on every version or only specific platforms?
  • Is it native support or a workaround through platform tools?
  • Are menus, combat, and UI prompts all designed around controller use?

Best Follow-Up

Open the deeper Controller Support Guide for the longer checklist, then compare with Release Date and Pre-Register if you are deciding where to play.

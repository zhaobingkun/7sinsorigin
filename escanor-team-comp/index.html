Escanor Team Comp Guide

Escanor team comps work best when the account already supports a real damage shell. He is easier to overvalue than to use well, because his headline pressure only pays off when the rest of the lineup protects uptime, rotation, and survival.

Best Escanor Team Rules

  • Do not force Escanor into a roster that still lacks basic support structure
  • Pair him with units that create reliable damage windows, not competing ego picks
  • Treat him as a centerpiece only if your current shell can keep him online consistently

What Official Context Tells Us

Netmarble highlighted Escanor in Version 1.2 promotion and also attached Potential Point shop support to his Hero Pick Up. That tells you he is meant to be a high-interest build target, but not automatically a plug-and-play answer for weak rosters.

When Escanor Fits Best

  • Accounts with stable support and enough resource depth to protect a damage core
  • Players who already solved early progression and are now optimizing clear speed or boss pressure
  • Teams that can capitalize on his windows instead of leaving him exposed between cycles

When to Avoid Forcing Him

  • When your beginner shell is still weak and unstable
  • When pulling Escanor delays the support pieces your account actually needs
  • When weekly raid value depends more on reliability than on peak burst

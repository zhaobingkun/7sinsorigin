Seven Deadly Sins Origin Weekly Checklist

Weekly-checklist pages matter because strong accounts are built around bigger rotation goals, not just daily maintenance. Use this page when you need to connect weekly resets, event windows, and banner timing to one plan.

What to Review Weekly

  • Any capped weekly rewards you still have not claimed
  • Event windows that expire before the next weekly cycle
  • Whether banner or currency priorities changed after the latest update

Why This Page Exists

Players often understand dailies but still lose efficiency on longer resets. Weekly planning is where account direction becomes clearer: save, invest, reroll, or shift into a new event objective.

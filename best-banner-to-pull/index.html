Best Banner to Pull On

The current banner to compare first is Elizabeth in Version 1.6. The right answer still depends on what your account is missing, how stable the featured unit is across teams, and whether spending now damages your next-cycle plan.

Current Short Answer

Start with Elizabeth. Pull only if her healer, Wind support, or Earth burst routes fix your account's current Version 1.6 needs; save if she is only the newest SSR on a roster that already functions.

How to Decide

  • Pull for missing account function first: carry, support glue, raid stability, or survival
  • Prefer banners that solve more than one team problem
  • Do not ignore future-banner risk if a better fit is later confirmed

When Saving Is Better

If Elizabeth only adds a niche upgrade while your account already clears current content, saving is usually the better move. That is especially true when pity and diamonds are under pressure after the 1.5 cycle.

Fast Compare Rules

  • Pick Elizabeth when she repairs a weak support, survival, or burst lane
  • Pick saving when your account is stable and no confirmed next banner fits yet
  • Skip both for now if the banner does not change progression enough to justify diamond pressure

What the “best” banner usually means

The best banner is usually the one that changes your account's daily and weekly output, not the one with the highest short-term excitement. That means broader team fit, safer pity use, and fewer wasted upgrade materials.

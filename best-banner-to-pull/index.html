Best Banner to Pull On

The right banner is rarely “the newest one.” The practical answer depends on what your account is missing, how stable the featured unit is across teams, and whether a better banner is likely close behind.

How to Decide

  • Pull for missing account function first: carry, support glue, or survival
  • Prefer banners that solve more than one team problem
  • Do not ignore upcoming-banner risk if a better fit may be next

When Saving Is Better

If a current banner only adds a niche upgrade while an upcoming unit may become a broader core piece, saving is usually the better move. That is especially true before the meta fully settles.

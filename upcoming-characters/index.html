Upcoming Characters

This page is for the watchlist side of the roster: units that are likely to matter next, characters tied to recent reveals, and names worth tracking before banner or patch confirmation is final.

What Counts as Upcoming

  • Newly teased characters in official news or patch notes
  • Characters strongly implied by banner or story reveals
  • Units likely to change banner-planning decisions once confirmed

Why Players Search This

Upcoming-character pages help players decide whether to save, wait, or keep materials flexible. These pages work best when they stay connected to news and banner pages instead of pretending every rumor is final.

Best Follow-Up Pages

Use this with Upcoming Banners, News, and the existing character guides when a teased unit already overlaps with current roster planning.

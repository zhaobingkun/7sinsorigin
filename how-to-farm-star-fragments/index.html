How to Farm Star Fragments

The current answer is not “spam one daily node forever.” Developer Notes #13 position Raid as the new regular Star Fragment route, with about 1,000 Star Fragments per week being prepared through the weekly reward loop.

Current Best Route

  • Prepare for weekly Raid instead of treating Star Fragments like a pure daily grind
  • Build one reliable team that can convert limited raid clears into real weekly income
  • Use checklist planning so your capped reward attempts are not wasted on underbuilt runs

What Official Notes Confirm

Developer Notes #13 say Raid is a weekly content mode, rewards are limited to two times per week, and the team is preparing roughly 1,000 Star Fragments each week through this route.

What to Prioritize First

  • Stable weekly-clear team before broader roster experiments
  • Weapon and growth upgrades that directly improve raid consistency
  • Weekly prep discipline over random midweek spending

What Not to Do

  • Do not assume old dungeon habits remain the best fragment route
  • Do not waste limited weekly reward chances on unstable team tests
  • Do not split resources so widely that none of your raid lanes are ready in time

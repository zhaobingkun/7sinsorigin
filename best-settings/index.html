Best Settings Guide

A useful settings page does not pretend there is one perfect preset for everyone. It should explain what to change first for performance, visibility, controls, and battery or heat tradeoffs.

What to Tune First

  • Frame-rate and visual-quality balance
  • Control sensitivity and camera comfort
  • Battery, heat, and stability tradeoffs for longer sessions

Who This Page Helps Most

This page is especially useful for mobile-first players, players moving between devices, and anyone trying to stabilize performance before launch-week grinding begins.

Best Companion Pages

Settings decisions connect directly to Combat System, Controller Support, and the install or update troubleshooting pages if performance feels wrong after setup.

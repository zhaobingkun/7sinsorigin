Merlin Team Comp Guide for Seven Deadly Sins Origin

The best Merlin team comp is the one where she has a clear job. Do not force Merlin into every team just because she is the current Version 1.5 SSR.

Direct Answer

A safe Merlin team uses one main carry, Merlin in a defined utility or pressure slot, one support or sustain slot, and one flex slot for the fight. This structure is safer than building four damage-first characters with no stability.

Stable Merlin Shell

  • Main carry: the unit your account can already support well.
  • Merlin: utility, pressure, or control depending on what her current build provides.
  • Support: sustain, buff, or defensive insurance.
  • Flex: raid mechanic answer, element fit, or secondary damage.

Raid Comp Logic

For raid, Merlin should improve weekly clear reliability. If adding her makes the team more fragile, keep her as a secondary route until your gear and support core are strong enough.

Beginner Comp Logic

Newer accounts should not rebuild the whole roster around Merlin immediately. Put her beside your most reliable carry and support first, then test whether she actually speeds up progression or just consumes scarce materials.

When Merlin Should Sit Out

  • Your current carry loses too much support when Merlin enters.
  • The fight needs a specific mechanic answer she does not provide.
  • Your Merlin is underbuilt and becomes the easiest failure point in longer content.

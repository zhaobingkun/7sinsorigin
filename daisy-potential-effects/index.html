Daisy Potential Effects Explained

Daisy's Potential matters more now because Netmarble explicitly revisited it in Developer Notes #13. That moves this from a niche detail into a live roster question, especially for players deciding whether Daisy deserves current investment.

Official Tier 6 Clarification

Developer Notes #13 say Daisy's Potential [Tier 6] effect will apply through Version 1.5 as follows: landing a Normal Attack or Ultimate Move decreases the enemy's Crit Defense by 15% for 20 seconds.

Why This Matters

  • It turns Daisy's Potential discussion into a live performance issue, not just old banner trivia
  • Crit Defense reduction creates clearer value when the rest of the team can convert that debuff into stable damage
  • It raises the value of understanding Daisy as a team-support decision, not just a name on a banner

How to Read the Effect

The key question is not whether a debuff exists. The real question is whether your June team can exploit it consistently. If your roster already has damage lanes that benefit from longer pressure windows, Daisy's Potential becomes easier to justify.

When It Matters Most

  • Accounts that need more team-enabled damage rather than one more isolated carry
  • Players deciding between Daisy utility and a future hard-carry pivot
  • Raid and weekly content where consistency matters more than one burst turn

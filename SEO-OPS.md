# SEO Ops - 7sinsorigin.com

## Primary Keyword Cluster
- seven deadly sins origin
- the seven deadly sins origin
- the seven deadly sins: origin
- 7ds origin

## Publishing Rhythm (Weekly)
- 1 news update in `/news/`
- 1 guide refresh in `/beginner-guide/`
- 1 meta update pass for top landing pages

## On-Page Standards
- Keep one clear intent per page
- Maintain `title` + `description` + `canonical`
- Keep `OG` and `Twitter` tags aligned with page intent
- Ensure every page has JSON-LD (`WebPage`, `Article`, or `FAQPage`)

## Technical Checklist
- `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` must stay updated
- Update all `<lastmod>` values after significant edits
- Keep EN/ZH `hreflang` pairs synced

## Measurement Loop
1. Review Google Search Console query report weekly
2. Improve pages with high impressions but low CTR
3. Expand internal links from home/news to guide pages
4. Refresh top 3 pages with latest patch or event context

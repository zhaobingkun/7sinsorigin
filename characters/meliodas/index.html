Current read: Current public team examples point to a Darkness burst finisher identity.

Meliodas currently shows up as the visible centerpiece of the Darkness setup, where the team appears to build a sequence first and then cash out through his burst window.

Why This Character Matters

The important question for Meliodas is not whether he is recognizable. It is whether his launch value comes from being a true account-defining carry or from being a high-payoff finisher that needs more structure around him.

Use this page when you need to decide if Darkness is worth pursuing early, or when you want to compare Meliodas against the safer frontline and support options already covered elsewhere on the site.

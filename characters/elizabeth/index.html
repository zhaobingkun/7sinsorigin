Character Snapshot

Elizabeth

Current read: Elizabeth is a Version 1.6 SSR with healer, support, and burst-route utility depending on weapon choice.

Adventure Skill: Goddess's Blessing recovers team HP outside combat and can trigger a one-time emergency recovery when team HP falls very low in combat.

Official descriptions may change before or at release; in-game text remains the final source.

Weapon Routes

  • Grimoire - Holy Warden: defensive route with damage reduction, healing over time, and a Splashing Around debuff that lowers enemy Defense.
  • Staff - Wind Support: Wind-team route with Weathering, Wind Attack support, Magic Consumption reduction, and Rupture utility.
  • Wand - Earth Burst Specialist: Earth burst route with cooldown reset, Burst Gauge support, and Earth Crit Chance/Pierce support.

Best Use Case

Elizabeth looks most important for accounts that need survival support, Wind support structure, or an Earth burst enabler. Skip early hype judgments until players test her uptime, cooldown pressure, and how much each route changes real raid and event reliability.

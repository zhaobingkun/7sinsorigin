Diane Guide and Character Overview

Diane stands out as one of the safest frontline-oriented names to watch for early progression because her profile naturally fits accounts that want stability, simple role clarity, and a clear main-carry identity.

Character Snapshot

Diane

Weapon tags: Axe, Gauntlets, Nunchaku

Quote: "Leave my friends alone!"

Falsely accused of treason and forced to live in hiding within the Forest of White Dreams, Diane still stands to protect the people she cares about.

Why This Character Matters

If you are building around Diane, the usual question is not just damage ceiling. It is whether your account wants a grounded frontline carry that can anchor safer teams while your supports fill in burst, defense, or utility windows.

Use this page when you are deciding whether Diane should become your first heavily invested unit, or when you want to compare her role against the lineups listed in Best Team Comps and the broader account-value view on the Tier List.

Role Notes

  • Best viewed as a stable frontline carry or bruiser-style centerpiece
  • Works best when the rest of the lineup protects her uptime or adds clean burst windows
  • More attractive for players who value consistency over narrow combo setups
